About

Hey there! I’m Ray. I am a solution-oriented developer student with a passion for impactful technology in society. I have had an interest in technology ever since childhood, and sitting glued in front of the computer screen before starting to lose my baby teeth was not something unusual. That interest followed me all the way to the university, where it instead became my field of study.

A few years later and with a lot more life experience, a move down to Malmö for the sake of love, and a background as an accountant under my belt, I have found my previous passion again. This time I chose to study a more practical education, where I also get the chance to learn what it is like to work within IT, with the help of internships.

Besides coding, I like to cook and eat food. Together with my girlfriend, our goal is to explore the unexplored in the world of food. I love to travel, and most recently I was in Asia in 2019 and backpacked for a two-month period to experience culture and above all to expose our taste buds to flavours that hit the right spot. Other than that, I like to spend time on casual multiplayer games or just lift scrap metal at the gym.

If you found something interesting about me, feel free to get in touch with me!
